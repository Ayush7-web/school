About Us

HOLY ANGELS' ENGLISH SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL was established in 2002 with a mission to inspire students to achieve their full potential. We provide a holistic learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities, a highly qualified teaching staff, and a focus on both academic excellence and personal growth.

Our school offers a diverse range of extracurricular activities, sports, and cultural events to ensure that every child has the opportunity to explore their interests and talents.
